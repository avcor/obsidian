
### Marshes, Bogs, Swamps, and other Messes 
Code messes wont stop you, but definitely slows you down. Product and Companies can be ruined because of this. It has prolonged effect on software, and it is worst. 
##### Why it happens 
- You create a solution for a simple problem, it works. Later on problem grow you extends the code base, trying to keep clean. 
- At some point you realize that you made wrong design decision and your code does not scale well. **Infecting Point**. This point you need to go back and fix design. 
- Professionals fear messes more than [[#Blind Alleys]]. 
- If you are moving forward with mess, you are lying to yourself, company, customer, that everything is going well, but you are moving toward doom.
