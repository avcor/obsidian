
## A Language of Commitment 

- 3 parts of commitment
	 1. You say you will do it 
	 2. You mean it 
	 3. You actually do it 

* Recognizing Lack of Commitment 
	* Words / Phrases to tell lack of commitment 
		1. Need / Should : We need to get this thing done 
		2. Hope / Wish : I hope to get this done by tomorrow
		3. Lets : Lets meet sometime 
	- What does commitment sounds like ^what-is-commitment
		1. I will ... by ...
		2. Binary result you get it done or not. If done people entrust you with your commitment else it will feel bad about not doing it. 
		
		- **It wouldn't work because it rely on other person to get it done**
			- commit to specific actions that will bring you closer to target. 
		- **It wouldn't work because I do not know if it can be done**
			- commit to actions that brings closer to target. Instead of solving 25 bugs, sit with QA and understand them, spend week getting closer to target. 
		- **It would not work because sometimes I just won't make it**
			- better to change expectation as soon as possible 
			- If you are not telling potential problem ASAP, you are not giving anyone a chances to help you with commitment
