
## Programmer vs People 
#### Programmer vs Employers
- Keep an eye on the goals who pay you 
- First need of professional programmer is to meet the need of employer. 
  Which means collaborating with managers, business analyst, tester and other teams to *to deeply understand business objectives*.
  It means that you need to know why are you writing this code and how business gaining benefit from it.
###### Transgression 
There was demo on Monday morning, It was a big day for company. Author left the state of software in non-functioning state last Friday, on top of that he came late on Monday an hour late. When asked why software is not working, he had no clue. Author did not care that it is important day for company. 
When asked "What if they had came", he still did not understand the importance of that day. Later on he was fired. Even he was best programmer out there in the company.
