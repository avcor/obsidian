Identification 
1. Identify 10 high performer by studying performance during last quarter of previous year. 
   Eliminate these stocks 
2. Focus on loser. Eliminate bottom 50% of performer during last of the previous year. 
3. Now left with correct stock list 

Selection 
1. Yearly tracking by observing yearly opening price 
2. Monthly opening. From month to month, you want to know if price are moving up or down. Use monthly price to form a trend line. 
3. Track weekly opening. 
4. Using simple procedure, you can look at trend line and get a good idea of overall health of stock

After that you can use short term strategies like ATR, beta etc.

Another big resistance is yearly high. 
Determine profit target by identifying the points of resistance level over 3 to 6 six month period.
