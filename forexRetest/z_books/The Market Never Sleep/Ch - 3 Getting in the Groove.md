## Getting with beat: Understand relationship 

| Relationship Chart                                                       | These relationships are general pattern of short term basis, and this list may not correctly reflect pattern of all market all times |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Interest Rate Rises                                                      | Equities Fall <br>Bond Prices Fall<br>Commodities Fall <br>Dollar Strengthens                                                        |
| Interest Rate Decline                                                    | Equities rise <br>Housing Investments Rise <br>Dollar Weakens                                                                        |
| Inflation increases                                                      | Equities fall<br>Bond prices fall <br>Gold prices rise                                                                               |
| Economic indicators <br>(GDP, CPI, PPI, housing starts, employment Data) | Strong data move market up<br>Weak data move them down                                                                               |
| Uncertainty <br>(politics or economic)                                   | Equities stall or fall <br>Bonds stall or fall <br>Commodities stall or fall <br>Gold rises                                          |
