### Use index futures as predictors 
Cash market follows future market.
Beware - if S&P, Dow and Dax are moving up but Nasdaq is not same, then check your analysis before taking long. It is a warning signal that move lack strength and prices may at reversal point or at correction point. 

### Statistical Indicators 
Author track NYSE Tick, TRIN or Arms Index, issues on both NYSE and Nasdaq

NYSE Tick 
number of stock ticking up as compared to ticking down. E.g. : +500 means 500 more stock than tick down or -700 means 700 more stock are ticking down than ticking up. 
500 to be noteworthy bullish -500 vice versa 
- You do not want to enter market when tick is too high wait it to settle and then tick and other indicator remain strong strong, become buyer 

Issues 1
Numerical data reflect number of issues listed on NY exchange that ticking up compared to ticking on previous day close
This indicator differ from NYSE tick in terms of data which offer slight longer view because they are bases on previous day close not latest tick. *It is a short term indicator* 

TRIN 
Arms Index or TRIN. Short Term lagging trading indicator. 
A = (Advancing issue / Declining Issues) 
B = (Advancing volume / Declining volume ) 
A/B
< 0.5 -> Bullish 
= 1.0 -> Neutral reading 
<= 2.0 -> Bearish & 3.0 might signal reversal or correction. (2.0 = 1000 on NYSE Tick)

### Market-Specific Indicators to watch 
Some economic situation and market segment represent the overall market direction. 

Petroleum 
EIA releases petroleum status report on Wednesday. Report contains both domestic and foreign petroleum inventories. 

Gold and Precious Metals
In case of geopolitical thread watch if 2 precious metals (Au and Ag) are in agreement this represent that something is afoot.
