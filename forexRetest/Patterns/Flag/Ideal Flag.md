### Premise  
- Sharp advance in price
- Large body
- Weak pullback in opp direction 
- High Volume 
- Break Trend line or S/R
- Flag must in opposite direction

### Entry Point
- When it breaks the flag in Market direction 

### Conditions
- Market In the direction of flag  
	- Bull -> Rising market 
	- Bear -> Falling market 
- Best in on-Time 

### Things to look out 
- Don't take part in going opp direction 
- [[Flag Delayed]]

### Take profit 
- Length of pole 

### Stop loss 
- According to flag 
	- Lowest point -> Bull Flag 
	- Highest point -> Bear Flag
