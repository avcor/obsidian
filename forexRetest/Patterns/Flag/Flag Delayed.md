### Premise  
-  Similar to [[Ideal Flag]]
- Price broke into segments (3), 
	- 1st large 
	- 2nd indecision 
	- 3rd large same as 1st
- **Flag**
	- Flag can go down almost half of pole 
	- Bull -> Initially rising then going down 
	- Bear -> Initially falling then going up
### Conditions
- This could happen during off-time with high volume
- Market In the direction of flag  
	- Bull -> Rising market 
	- Bear -> Falling market 

### Things to look out 
- Might reach to where it started 
- It will take time to reach the target price 

### Take profit 
- Max/Min of flag 
- Length of Pole 

### Stop loss 
- According to flag 
	- Lowest point -> Bull Flag 
	- Highest point -> Bear Flag
